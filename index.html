Hello! My name Is Jared and I'm currently enrolled in a bootcamp program at the U of O to learn coding. My goal through this bootcamp is to completely change my career path. For nearly seven years I worked in the food industry as a cook/kitchen manager even though I knew I didn't want to do that for the rest of my life. My dream is to work in the games industry as my favorite hobby is gaming. I've spent nearly my entire life around computers and I've known for a while that I could always spend an endless amount of time learning as much as I can about them.

Run Buddy

Run Buddy is an assignment that I worked on for a couple of weeks through the U of O boot camp. The first week of this assignment introduced basic HTML and CSS structure while also showing me basic commands for updating and commiting my work through git. The second week taught me a lot about debugging while also showing me more advanced tricks in CSS, like media queries and flexbox. At the same time I learned more advanced Git commands like feature branches, pulling, and merging work with other developers.
